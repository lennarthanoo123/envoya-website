FAQ

Everything you want to know about Envoya

Isn't this just an expensive spam tool?

No. Spam is sending the same message to 10,000 people. Our system sends each person a message based on data about that specific person. That's the difference between 0.3% and 12% reply rate.

What if it doesn't work for us?

Then we fix it. We're so confident in our system that we don't equate results with no deal for us. Full stop.

How quickly do we see results?

First campaign live within 14 days. First responses within 30 days. Average 3 new meetings in week 1 of the campaign.

Do we need to change our sales process?

No. Lead Machine fits around your existing workflow. You still do the conversations and closes. We just fill your calendar.

How is this different from hiring an SDR?

An BDR costs €40–60K/year, needs onboarding, gets sick and quits. Lead Machine costs €25K once, runs 24/7, and gets smarter every week.

Is it really AI, or just mail merge?

Really AI. Every email has a personalised hook based on the recipient's recent interviews, company news or role context. Pattern A/B/C email selection, self-learning ICP weights, real-time signal research.
